The Pony Music Archive

About

The Pony fandom has produced an amazing amount of music of all kinds, welcome to its largest, organized, high-quality music archive!
The September 2020 release includes 50,000 tracks by 3,300 artists and 250 compilation albums.

The show may have ended, but the music side of the fandom is not ready to slow down just yet! This year brings one of our largest updates, with a healthy mix of brand new tracks and old archived content from the early years of the fandom.

You can consider this project a spiritual successor to the MLP Music Archive, but actively maintained and gathering songs from the best sources available. The archive updates every six months, so make sure to check back on September 1st for the next release!

Download

The High Quality version is recommended if you just want to listen.
It's designed so that you shouldn't hear the difference with the Raw version.
If you're short on space, Phone Quality is very compact and still sounds fine.

Because browsers can't download this much in one go, we use Torrents for the archive. As a bonus, this allow you to pick and choose what folders you want to download.
If you don't already have a Torrent client, try one of these: qBittorrent, PicoTorrent, or Deluge.
