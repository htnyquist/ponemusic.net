The Pony Music Archive - Contribute

Contributions are welcome!


Despite our best efforts the Pony Music Archive is still incomplete.
You can help make sure nothing is lost to time by submitting any missing content you find!
If you notice a musician has been overlooked, a song is missing, or any other issue with the archive that you can't submit a file for, please don't hesitate to reach out to the archive's maintainers at htnyquist@gmail.com.

Submit content

We accept WAV, FLAC, ALAC, MP3, OGG, and OPUS audio files. Making a ZIP file is also okay.
Submissions are limited to 50 files and 10GB per IP and must contain music suitable for the archive.
Please understand that we will not accept songs from artists that have requested to be excluded.
Uploads are reviewed by the maintainers and gratuitous abuse may result in an IP ban.
